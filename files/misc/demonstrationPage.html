Welcome to the City of Oras Nou Tourism Information Website. This element is a container. Containers hold centered text and max out at 1400 pixels in width. They will remain centered.

You can also use leftContainer in the class section for text that is not centered in the container. The container will still be centered on the page once it exceeds a width of 1400px.

This is a Column Container

Column containers are used when you want to display text alongside an image. The text and image will scale together, unless the image's height is taller than that of the text.
